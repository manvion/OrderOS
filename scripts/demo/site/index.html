About us

We have been griddling burgers on this corner for fifteen years. This website was built in 2014 by the owner's nephew, and we are not rebuilding it.

We are, however, tired of handing thirty percent of every delivery order to a marketplace app.

This is the whole point. Nothing about this page changed except one <script> tag before </body>. The ordering button in the corner, the menu, the cart, the checkout and the order tracking all come from OrderOS — and the customer never leaves this website.
